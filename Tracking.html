Intelligent Power Management System (IPMS): Real-Time Power Optimization and Automation

Overview

Meghalaya Tourism embarked on a digital transformation journey to create an integrated tourism solution that benefits all stakeholders. Xlayer developed Dtour, a robust suite empowering tourists and service providers alike, simplifying travel experiences and enabling smarter operations.

Featured Product

Dtour

A cloud-based digital tourism platform that enables seamless travel planning, booking, and service connectivity through powerful apps and tools.

Services

Web Development App Development UI/UX Enhancement

Deliverables

Key Automation Features Chatbots Payment Integration

The Ask

Meghalaya Tourism aimed to build an all-in-one digital tourism suite to streamline services for tourists and providers. It had to reflect post-pandemic expectations, offering scalable solutions, real-time data, and seamless booking experiences.

Solution

Xlayer delivered a suite that streamlines discovery, booking, support, and feedback. From a unified mobile app to admin dashboards, it connects the government, service providers, and tourists on one powerful platform.

Technologies

Angular Typescript Python MongoDB Flutter

Impact

Tourists are now able to confidently search, plan and book services on this highly credible state backed platform. They are able to seamlessly avail services built on this AI enabled solution. For the service providers, the suite helps to establish credible online presence, bringing down operational costs resulting in better inventory discovery, higher occupancy and better engagement with tourists. Availability of exhaustive tourist data helps the government in planning for peak seasons and mega events. It helps the state devising focused marketing campaigns and improving the overall tourism experience in Meghalaya.
